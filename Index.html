IZibusiso Farm is an independent business in KwaZulu Natal owned by Fikile Nkosi. The farm specializes in selling eggs and cabbage. They are dedicated to bringing eggs and cabbage closer to the community and offering fair prices. Their aim is to establish a recognized farm by maintaining quality and consistency in their concept through effective marketing and operational execution.

The Land

Owning land in South Africa offers a multitude of possibilities for exploration and investment. From its diverse landscapes to its rich cultural heritage, the country's land presents numerous opportunities.South Africa's land offers a chance to immerse oneself in its vibrant multicultural society, as it provides the opportunity to build a home or establish a business that fosters connections with local communities and contributes to economic growth.

Poutry

Poultry farming brings significant benefits to the community by creating employment opportunities, especially in rural areas, where job opportunities may be limited. It offers jobs in various areas, including farming, processing, distribution, and sales, contributing to local economic growth. Poultry farming promotes food security within the community by providing a reliable source of nutritious protein in the form of eggs and poultry meat.
